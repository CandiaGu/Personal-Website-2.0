A little about me

I’m an electrical and computer engineering student at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Although I do love engineering, I’ve done art since I was kid, if you count the scribbly mess that were my works. And now, I combine both art and engineering to make cool stuff…or something like that. Please enjoy my pieces as much as I did making them. :D
