Digital

{% elif pic=='kokedama' %}

Kokedama Festival

Digital

This is a poster I designed for my Document Design class in which I had to promote a very specific aspect of urban gardening. I used very brightly colored elements to make the event seem very approachable and fun. The bubblyness of the Japanese characters not only promoted the culture of the event, but also added to the childlike aspect of the event.

To summarize, kokedama is a type of mossball that people can used to grow other plants out of and is very aesthetically pleasing. It is often hung on strings as display, hence the line coming in and out of the center sphere.
